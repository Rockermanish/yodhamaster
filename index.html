Kata.

KATA is also symbolized as the shadow fight. It is a shadow form of fight in which you fight against an imaginable opponent depicted by shadow of the person performing KATA. It describes the precise movement of every part of the body with a specific stance and precise movement of body by maintaining the stance and body posture

Kumite means Sparring, and is one of the three main parts of Karate Training, along with Kata and Kihon.Kumite is when you train against an adversary, using the techniques learnt from the Kihon and Kata.Since the word “ Kumite” refers to forms of Sparring.
